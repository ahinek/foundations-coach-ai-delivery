# Foundations Coach for AI Delivery

This repository outlines a minimal, principle-driven view of a role I’ve been calling **Foundations Coaching for AI Delivery**.

It is not a framework.  
It is not a certification.  
It is not a replacement for Scrum Masters or Agile Coaches.

It is a starting point.

---

## Why this exists

AI is now embedded in how teams deliver software. When something that powerful becomes ubiquitous, the foundations beneath it matter more than ever.

This repository describes guardrails, signals, and responsibilities that help ensure AI magnifies the best qualities of how teams work together — rather than amplifying fragmentation.

---

## What this emphasizes

Acceleration is powerful only when built on:

- Shared guardrails
- Shared tooling ecosystems
- Shared product clarity
- Shared accountability
- Preserved team cohesion

---

## Who this is for

- Scrum Masters
- Agile Coaches
- Engineering Managers
- Product Leaders
- Delivery Leaders
- Anyone stewarding AI adoption inside teams

---

## Repository structure

- `ROLE_PURPOSE.md` — why this role exists
- `CORE_PRINCIPLES.md` — foundational guardrails
- `RESPONSIBILITIES.md` — observable responsibilities
- `LEADER_ALIGNMENT.md` — what leadership must reinforce
- `SIGNALS_TO_WATCH.md` — early indicators of fragmentation
- `NOT_THIS.md` — what this role is not
- 'TRY_THE_EXPERIMENT.md' — start small and measure well

Minimal by design. Expand thoughtfully.
